About me

As a technology enthusiast I have a passion for computers and all things tech, web development is a natural progression for me that joins this love for science and technology, this site exists to promote my work, and to provide potential clients with a means to contact me for consultation.



My experience

When I set out to become a web developer, I had a single goal in mind, to learn web development from the ground up, no third party software and no website building applications, I focused almost entirely on HTML, CSS and Javascript, the three core programming languages upon which the world wide web is built, this fundamental knowledge gives me the skills and know how that is necessary to create robust, responsive, bug free software that works perfectly on desktop, tablet or mobile.
